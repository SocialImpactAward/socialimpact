Nord Anglia Education x UNICEF

The NAE Social Impact Award with UNICEF was first launched in 2021 and is currently piloting in select NAE schools. It is derived from the UNICEF UK Rights Respecting Schools Award, but adapted for international schools. The original award focuses on putting children’s rights at the heart of schools and this is still the key element of the Social Impact Award. However, the NAE Education Team have worked directly with UNICEF to ensure that the Social Impact Award celebrates equality and diversity in international schools and make children’s rights a basis for intercultural understanding.
