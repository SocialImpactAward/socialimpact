the Social Impact Award

Nord Anglia Education x UNICEF

Watch this video

The Nord Anglia Social Impact Award is derived from the UNICEF Rights Respecting Schools Award that focuses on all students learning about their rights and respect towards others.

Following the UN Convention on the Rights of the Child, children learn all four key categories as they learn about rights.

Outcomes from the award

By the third stage of the Award, Gold, schools achieve positive performance across a broad range of outcomes, including:

Knowing rights

Children know about their rights and support the rights of others locally and globally.

happiness

Children feel happer in themselves.

safety

Children feel safe in school.

engagement

Children are engaged in their education.
